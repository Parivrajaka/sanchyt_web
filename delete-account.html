Delete Your Sanchyt Account

You can delete your account and all associated data directly from the Sanchyt app. No email or support request is needed.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Sanchyt app and sign in
  2. Go to the Settings tab (bottom navigation)
  3. Scroll down to the Data section
  4. Tap Delete My Data
  5. Type the confirmation text as shown on screen
  6. Tap Delete to confirm

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  1. Your user profile — name, email, role, and preferences
  2. Your expenses — all expenses you created
  3. Your Firebase Auth account — sign-in credentials

If you are the Admin and choose "Delete All Family Data", the following is also deleted:

  1. All family members' expenses
  2. All budgets and recurring expense settings
  3. The family group itself

What Is NOT Stored

Sanchyt does not store receipt images on any server. All OCR processing happens on your device. There are no images to delete.

Data Retention

Deletion is immediate and permanent. No data is retained after deletion. There is no recovery period.

Before You Delete

You can export all your data before deleting your account:

  1. Go to Settings → Export My Data to download your personal data (GDPR export)
  2. Go to Settings → Export Data to download expenses as CSV, JSON, or PDF
